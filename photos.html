DIGITAL PHOTOGRAPHY PROJECT

The goal of this project was to professionally take photos and use software to adjust and improve them.

To start we had to...
- Plan the composition of your photos page while you are taking the photos. You will use this information when writing your captions.
- Take several photos indoors and several photos outdoors using a digital camera.
- Adjust camera settings as needed to capture optimum lighting and resolution of images.
- Use various composition techniques (symmetry, rule of thirds, varied point of view, leading lines, framing)

After this we were responsible for...
- Resizing the image file to an appropriate size
- Open Adobe Photoshop
- Use Adobe Photoshop to adjust brightness, contrast, levels, tones, colors, etc.
- Crop the photo to an appropriate shape
- Add the edited and unedited photos into Adobe InDesign
- Professionally add them to a page using basic design priciples
